Targeted Advertising

We want to bring you right to your customers - Using our Hash Tag Matching Technology, we will connect your brand with potential and loyal customers. You will be able to design ads to showcase your new or existing product offering, sales or promotions. You will also be able to track engagements, conversions, click through rates and also get a precise mapping of where your loyal & potential customers are located within the city using our Heat Map technology.

Find & Engage With Loyal Customers

Reach your loyal customers wherever they are using our Rewards System. When someone becomes a Rainbow Member they get the opportunity to redeem a gift card or coupon from their favorite stores & brands. You can keep track of where your loyal customers are, stay up to date with them, direct traffic to the physical locations by rewarding them with a gift card or coupon, promote new products, and much more! This is also your chance to reach new customers. You can also get to learn more about your loyal or potential customer profiles.
